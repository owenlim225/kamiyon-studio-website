# Company Overview

> **Kamiyon Studio** is a Filipino creative technology company that creates games and interactive experiences designed to educate, inspire, and leave a lasting impact.

---

# Executive Summary

Kamiyon Studio is a multidisciplinary creative technology studio founded in **2024** in **Biñan City, Laguna, Philippines**. Originally formed by two game development students passionate about game jams and hackathons, the company has evolved into a team of designers, developers, artists, and creatives united by a shared belief: **world-class interactive experiences can be built in the Philippines.**

Today, Kamiyon Studio operates as both a **creative technology agency** and an **original IP studio**. Professional services provide the financial foundation that enables the studio to invest in developing its own games, educational platforms, and interactive technologies.

Kamiyon combines expertise in game development, software engineering, artificial intelligence, blockchain technologies, UI/UX, digital art, and educational design to create products that are both engaging and meaningful.

The studio's long-term ambition is to become a globally recognized multimedia entertainment company known for original intellectual properties, educational innovation, and Filipino creativity.

# What "Kamiyon" Means

The name **Kamiyon** comes from two simple words:

* **Kami** ("us")
* **'Yon** ("that")

Together, they express a simple but meaningful idea:

> **"That's us."**

The name reflects the belief that every project represents the team's identity, values, creativity, and craftsmanship. Rather than chasing trends or copying others, Kamiyon aims to create experiences that genuinely represent who they are as creators.

Every game, application, illustration, and product is an extension of the people who built it.

---

# Why Kamiyon Exists

Kamiyon Studio exists to prove that **world-class games and interactive experiences can be created in the Philippines.**

Beyond building products, the studio seeks to inspire aspiring Filipino creators by demonstrating that creativity, craftsmanship, and innovation are not limited by geography.

The company's success should create opportunities—not only for itself—but for future generations of Filipino developers, artists, designers, and entrepreneurs.

# Business Model

Kamiyon currently operates under a dual-track model.

## Client Services

Professional services generate sustainable revenue while allowing the team to solve real-world problems for organizations.

These services include game development, software development, design, consulting, gamification, AI integration, blockchain solutions, and interactive media.

---

## Original Products

Revenue from client work is reinvested into building Kamiyon's own intellectual properties.

This strategy allows the company to pursue ambitious long-term projects without relying solely on external funding.

The long-term objective is for original products and intellectual properties to become the company's primary source of revenue.

# Philosophy

Kamiyon believes that technology should always serve a purpose.

Games are not created simply to entertain.

AI is not adopted simply because it is popular.

Blockchain is not implemented simply because it is innovative.

Instead, every technology should contribute meaningfully to solving problems, improving experiences, or creating measurable impact.

The question is never:

> "What technology should we use?"

The question is:

> "What experience are we trying to create?"

However, true success is measured by:
