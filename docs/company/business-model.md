# Business Model

> **Kamiyon Studio is built on a sustainable dual-track business model: delivering professional services today while investing in the original intellectual properties of tomorrow.**

---

# Purpose

This document explains how Kamiyon Studio generates revenue, allocates resources, and balances short-term sustainability with long-term growth.

Our business model is intentionally designed to allow the company to remain financially healthy while continuously investing in original products that define our future.

---

# Business Philosophy

Kamiyon Studio does not view client services and original products as competing priorities.

Instead, they work together.

Client work provides stability, experience, industry relationships, and financial resources.

Original products build long-term value, brand recognition, and intellectual property.

Together, they create a business capable of sustainable growth.

---

# The Dual-Track Model

Kamiyon operates through two complementary divisions.

## 1. Creative Technology Services

Professional services generate recurring revenue by helping organizations design and build interactive digital experiences.

These projects expose the team to real-world challenges while strengthening technical expertise and expanding the company's network.

The agency side of the business exists to:

* Generate sustainable revenue.
* Build long-term partnerships.
* Improve technical capabilities.
* Expand the team's portfolio.
* Fund internal product development.

---

## 2. Original Products

Kamiyon invests agency revenue into developing its own products and intellectual properties.

These products represent the company's long-term vision and creative identity.

Unlike client projects, original products continue generating value beyond their initial development through updates, publishing, licensing, education, and future franchise opportunities.

---

# Why This Model?

Many creative studios struggle because they rely entirely on either client work or product development.

Kamiyon intentionally combines both.

### Client Services Provide

* Predictable cash flow.
* Industry relationships.
* Diverse technical experience.
* Portfolio growth.
* Market insight.

### Original Products Provide

* Long-term intellectual property.
* Creative freedom.
* Brand identity.
* Scalable revenue.
* Lasting company value.

Together, these strengths create a more resilient business.

---

# Investment Philosophy

Agency revenue should not simply become profit.

Whenever practical, profits should be reinvested into strengthening the company's future.

Examples include:

* Original game development.
* Team education.
* Software licenses.
* Research and development.
* Better development tools.
* Marketing.
* Community initiatives.
* Hiring.

We prioritize long-term value creation over maximizing short-term returns.

---

# Client Philosophy

We do not view clients as transactions.

We view them as partners.

The best partnerships are built on:

* Transparency.
* Communication.
* Mutual respect.
* Shared goals.
* Long-term collaboration.

Whenever possible, Kamiyon seeks to educate clients throughout the development process rather than simply delivering completed work.

---

# Product Investment Framework

Not every idea should become a product.

Before committing significant resources, we evaluate opportunities using several questions:

* Does this align with our mission?
* Does it solve a meaningful problem?
* Can it inspire, educate, or entertain?
* Does it strengthen our portfolio?
* Can we execute it at a high standard?
* Does it have long-term potential?

Projects that satisfy these criteria receive greater priority.

---

# Risk Management

As a growing studio, responsible risk management is essential.

Kamiyon manages risk by:

* Maintaining multiple revenue sources.
* Avoiding dependence on a single client.
* Investing gradually into original products.
* Building reusable internal tools.
* Documenting knowledge.
* Developing multidisciplinary skills within the team.

Sustainable growth is preferred over aggressive expansion.

# What We Will Never Become

Growth should never come at the expense of our identity.

Kamiyon will not:

* Chase technology trends without purpose.
* Accept projects that fundamentally conflict with our values.
* Sacrifice quality for rapid expansion.
* Neglect original product development in favor of short-term revenue.

Every opportunity should strengthen the company we aspire to become.
