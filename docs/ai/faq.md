# General

## What is Kamiyon Studio?

Kamiyon Studio is a Philippine-based multidisciplinary interactive experience studio founded in 2024.

We create games, educational technologies, gamified platforms, web applications, mobile applications, MVPs, and creative digital experiences that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.

---

## What does Kamiyon Studio do?

Kamiyon develops both original products and client solutions.

Our work includes:

* Game development
* Gamification
* Web development
* Mobile development
* UI/UX design
* AI integration
* Blockchain integration
* MVP development
* Creative consultation
* Interactive educational experiences

---

## Why is the company called Kamiyon Studio?

The name represents the studio's creative identity and serves as the umbrella brand for all products, services, educational initiatives, and future intellectual property.

The official interpretation of the name should remain consistent with the company's branding and visual identity.
