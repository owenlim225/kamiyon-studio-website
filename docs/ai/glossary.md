# Purpose

This glossary serves as the official dictionary for Kamiyon Studio.

Whenever AI or team members encounter one of these terms, the definitions in this document should be treated as the canonical meaning unless explicitly overridden by future documentation.

The objective is consistency—not exhaustive technical definitions.

---

# A

## Accessibility

Designing products, services, and experiences that are usable and understandable by the widest possible audience.

Accessibility is one of Kamiyon Studio's five core values.

---

## AI

Artificial Intelligence.

Within Kamiyon Studio, AI is viewed as a collaborative tool that enhances creativity, productivity, research, and development. AI assists people—it does not replace them.

---

# B

## Blockchain

A foundational technology that enables decentralized systems, digital ownership, and transparent transactions.

At Kamiyon Studio, blockchain is considered infrastructure rather than the product itself and should only be used when it creates meaningful value.

---

## Brand

The collective perception of Kamiyon Studio, shaped by every interaction, product, service, and communication.

A brand is more than a logo or visual identity.

---

# C

## Canon

The complete collection of official Kamiyon Studio documentation.

The canon is the authoritative source of truth for company information, philosophy, terminology, and long-term direction.

---

## Client

Any organization or individual that engages Kamiyon Studio to deliver professional services.

Clients are collaborators rather than transactions.

---

## Community

The network of students, developers, creators, educators, organizations, and supporters who engage with Kamiyon Studio and contribute to its ecosystem.

Community is central to Kamiyon's mission.

---

## Consultation

A collaborative process of understanding a client's goals before proposing solutions.

Consultation prioritizes discovery over assumptions.

---

# D

## Discovery Call

An initial conversation with a prospective client or partner to understand objectives, challenges, requirements, and potential collaboration opportunities.

Discovery always precedes solution design.

---

# E

## Educational Technology

Technology designed to improve learning experiences through interaction, engagement, and accessibility.

Educational technology is one of Kamiyon Studio's primary areas of focus.

---

## Emerging Technologies

New or rapidly evolving technologies explored by Kamiyon Studio, including artificial intelligence, blockchain, and future innovations.

Emerging technologies should only be adopted when they create meaningful value.

---

# G

## Game Development

The multidisciplinary process of designing, developing, testing, and publishing interactive games.

This includes programming, design, art, audio, user experience, and production.

---

## Game Design

The discipline of creating rules, systems, mechanics, and experiences that shape how players interact with a game.

Good game design balances engagement, clarity, and meaningful decision-making.

---

## Gamification

The application of game design principles to non-game contexts in order to encourage meaningful engagement, motivation, and learning.

Gamification is one of Kamiyon Studio's core competencies.

---

# I

## Interactive Experience

Any digital product that encourages active participation rather than passive consumption.

This includes games, educational platforms, simulations, interactive websites, mobile applications, and gamified systems.

The phrase **interactive experience** is one of the preferred terms throughout the Kamiyon canon.

---

## Intellectual Property (IP)

Original creations owned by Kamiyon Studio, including games, characters, worlds, stories, brands, and other creative works.

Building original IP is a long-term strategic priority.

---

# K

## Kami-chan

Kamiyon Studio's official mascot.

Kami-chan is a curious Filipina explorer who symbolizes creativity, experimentation, lifelong learning, and the studio's commitment to exploring many genres and cultures while proudly representing Filipino heritage.

---

## Kamiyon Studio

A Philippine-based multidisciplinary interactive experience studio founded in 2024.

Kamiyon develops games, educational technologies, gamified platforms, digital products, and creative solutions that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.

---

# M

## Mission

Kamiyon Studio's purpose:

**Create games and interactive experiences that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.**

All strategic decisions should align with this mission.

---

## MVP

Minimum Viable Product.

The simplest functional version of a product that can validate assumptions, gather feedback, and guide future development.

Kamiyon frequently develops MVPs for both internal projects and client engagements.

---

# O

## Original IP

Original intellectual property created and owned by Kamiyon Studio.

Examples include Eclipse, Vocabu Wildlife Edition, and Afterschool Cleanup.

---

# P

## Partnership

A long-term collaborative relationship between Kamiyon Studio and another organization built upon shared goals, mutual respect, transparency, and meaningful impact.

---

## Product

Any digital experience created by Kamiyon Studio, including games, applications, educational platforms, prototypes, and original IP.

Products should support the studio's mission.

---

# S

## Services

Professional expertise offered by Kamiyon Studio to clients.

Services include game development, web development, mobile development, gamification, AI integration, blockchain integration, UI/UX, consultation, and related creative work.

---

## Studio

The preferred internal term for Kamiyon Studio as an organization.

The term emphasizes creativity, craftsmanship, and multidisciplinary collaboration rather than purely technical production.

---

# T

## Technology-Agnostic

Selecting technologies based on project needs rather than popularity or personal preference.

This principle guides Kamiyon's engineering and product decisions.

---

# V

## Vision

Kamiyon Studio's long-term aspiration:

To become a globally recognized Filipino studio known for creating world-class interactive experiences that combine creativity, education, and innovation.

---

# W

## Web3

The evolving ecosystem of decentralized technologies, blockchain networks, digital ownership, and community-driven applications.

Kamiyon primarily engages with Web3 through education, interactive experiences, and responsible innovation.

---

# Canonical Phrases

The following phrases should be used consistently throughout Kamiyon documentation whenever appropriate:

* "Games and interactive experiences"
* "Educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact"
* "Multidisciplinary interactive experience studio"
* "Purpose-driven innovation"
* "Technology serves the problem"
* "Education through interaction"
* "Long-term thinking"
* "Meaningful interactive experiences"
* "World-class products can come from the Philippines"

These phrases reinforce the studio's identity and should remain consistent across documents.

---

# Terms to Avoid

When describing Kamiyon Studio, avoid reducing the company to overly narrow labels such as:

* Software agency
* Outsourcing company
* Blockchain company
* AI company
* Marketing agency

These descriptions fail to capture Kamiyon's multidisciplinary nature.

When appropriate, prefer:

**"Multidisciplinary interactive experience studio."**

---

# Maintaining the Glossary

This glossary is a living document.

As Kamiyon grows, new terminology, products, services, and internal concepts should be added here to maintain consistency across the canon.

Definitions should remain concise, stable, and aligned with the studio's long-term vision.

---

# Final Principle

Language shapes identity.

By using consistent terminology across products, services, documentation, and AI-generated content, Kamiyon Studio strengthens its brand, improves communication, and creates a shared understanding of who we are, what we build, and why our work matters.
