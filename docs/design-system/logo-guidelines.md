# Purpose

This document establishes the official guidelines for using the Kamiyon Studio logo.

Its purpose is to ensure that the logo remains recognizable, consistent, and visually effective across all physical and digital applications.

Every team member, partner, vendor, and AI system should follow these guidelines whenever the logo is used.

---

# Logo Overview

The Kamiyon Studio logo is built around a stylized **sakura blossom**.

The logo represents the studio's philosophy of:

* Creativity
* Growth
* Craftsmanship
* Curiosity
* Education
* The fusion of Filipino identity with Japanese-inspired design philosophy

The logo is intended to communicate both approachability and premium quality.

---

# Brand Meaning

The sakura blossom symbolizes:

* Continuous growth through learning.
* The beginning of new creative journeys.
* Curiosity and exploration.
* Beauty created through craftsmanship.
* Meaningful experiences that leave a lasting impression.

The logo is not merely decorative—it represents the values and aspirations of Kamiyon Studio.

---

# Primary Logo

The primary logo consists of:

* The official sakura icon.
* The "Kamiyon Studio" wordmark.
* Official brand colors.

Whenever sufficient space is available, the primary logo should be used.

---

# Secondary Variations

Approved secondary versions may include:

* Icon only.
* Wordmark only.
* Horizontal layout.
* Vertical layout.
* Monochrome version.
* White version for dark backgrounds.

No other variations should be created without approval.

---

# Minimum Size

The logo should always remain legible.

If the wordmark becomes difficult to read at smaller sizes, use the icon-only version instead.

Never reduce the logo below the point where important details become unclear.

---

# Clear Space

Maintain sufficient empty space around the logo at all times.

No text, graphics, or other visual elements should intrude into this area.

The clear space should preserve the logo's visibility and prevent visual clutter.

---

# Color Usage

Whenever possible, use the official brand colors.

Primary applications should follow the approved color palette.

Monochrome versions should only be used when color reproduction is impractical or intentionally limited.

Do not substitute unofficial colors.

---

# Backgrounds

The logo should always maintain strong contrast against its background.

Preferred backgrounds include:

* Warm ivory.
* White.
* Deep indigo.
* Charcoal.

Avoid backgrounds that reduce readability or compete with the logo.

---

# Scaling

The logo may be resized proportionally.

Never:

* Stretch it horizontally.
* Stretch it vertically.
* Distort its proportions.
* Compress the artwork.

Maintain the original aspect ratio at all times.

---

# Rotation

The logo should always remain upright.

Do not rotate, tilt, skew, or flip the logo unless an officially approved animated application requires it.

---

# Effects

Do not apply decorative effects such as:

* Heavy shadows.
* Unapproved outlines.
* Glows.
* Bevels.
* Embossing.
* Distortion.
* Unapproved gradients.

The logo should rely on its own design rather than additional styling.

---

# Placement

The logo may appear on:

* Websites.
* Games.
* Applications.
* Presentations.
* Social media.
* Merchandise.
* Printed materials.
* Event banners.
* Business documents.

Placement should prioritize visibility without overwhelming surrounding content.

---

# Relationship with Kami-chan

The logo and Kami-chan are complementary brand assets.

Kami-chan should never replace the logo as the official identifier of Kamiyon Studio.

When used together:

* The logo represents the organization.
* Kami-chan represents the personality of the brand.

Each should maintain its own distinct role.

---

# Co-Branding

When displayed alongside partner logos:

* Maintain equal visual respect.
* Preserve clear spacing.
* Avoid overlapping graphics.
* Use approved logo variations.
* Follow any applicable partnership branding agreements.

The Kamiyon logo should remain recognizable without dominating collaborative materials.

---

# Incorrect Usage

The following uses are not permitted:

* Changing the official colors.
* Redrawing the logo.
* Altering the proportions.
* Rotating or skewing the logo.
* Placing the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.
* Adding decorative effects.
* Combining the logo with unofficial graphics.
* Modifying the icon or wordmark independently without an approved variation.

Consistency strengthens recognition.

---

# Accessibility

When using the logo in digital environments:

* Ensure sufficient color contrast.
* Provide alternative text where appropriate.
* Preserve clarity at various screen sizes.
* Use high-resolution assets.

Accessibility supports a wider audience and reflects Kamiyon's commitment to inclusive design.

---

# Future Evolution

The logo should evolve only when there is a significant strategic reason.

Minor refinements may occur over time to improve usability or modernize execution, but changes should preserve the recognizable identity of the mark.

Brand recognition is built through consistency.

---

# Relationship to the Brand

The logo is the visual signature of Kamiyon Studio.

It represents every product, service, partnership, and community initiative created by the studio.

Its consistent use reinforces trust, professionalism, and long-term brand recognition.

---

# Final Principle

The Kamiyon Studio logo is more than a graphic.

It is the visual embodiment of the studio's mission, values, and aspirations.

Every use of the logo should reflect the same care, craftsmanship, and attention to detail that Kamiyon Studio brings to every interactive experience it creates.
