# About Kamiyon Studio

**Kamiyon Studio** is a Filipino creative technology studio founded in **2024** that creates games and interactive experiences designed to spark curiosity, encourage exploration, and inspire people to create.

Rather than viewing games as entertainment alone, Kamiyon sees play as one of the most powerful ways to explore ideas, understand different perspectives, and make complex topics approachable. By combining storytelling, psychology, culture, technology, and thoughtful design, the studio creates experiences that remain engaging while leaving players with new questions to explore.

Today, Kamiyon provides professional creative and technical services to organizations while building toward a future centered around original intellectual properties and globally recognized interactive experiences.

# Core Philosophy

Kamiyon believes that **play is one of humanity's most powerful tools for understanding the world.**

The world's most meaningful questions rarely have simple answers. Rather than telling people what to think, Kamiyon creates interactive experiences that encourage players to explore different perspectives, ask better questions, and arrive at their own understanding.

Technology is never the destination—it is the medium.

Games are more than entertainment—they are spaces for curiosity, experimentation, and discovery.

Kamiyon believes that the most original ideas often emerge where different cultures, disciplines, technologies, and perspectives intersect.

Every project should leave people more curious than when they began.
