# Vision

Afterschool Cleanup seeks to transform an ordinary after-school cleaning routine into an unsettling psychological thriller.

The game is designed around a simple premise:

The player is assigned to clean after school.

As the routine progresses, small irregularities begin to appear.

Through observation, exploration, and environmental storytelling, players slowly uncover a deeper narrative hidden beneath familiar surroundings.

The experience should create tension through atmosphere rather than constant action.

---

# Core Concept

The primary gameplay loop combines:

* Cleaning.
* Exploration.
* Observation.
* Environmental storytelling.
* Psychological suspense.

The player's responsibilities remain familiar and grounded, allowing subtle changes in the environment to become increasingly impactful.

The contrast between ordinary tasks and growing unease forms the emotional foundation of the game.

---

# Core Gameplay Pillars

## Relaxing Routine

Cleaning should feel naturally satisfying.

Players should experience the calm rhythm of completing everyday tasks before suspense gradually emerges.

The ordinary establishes the emotional baseline for everything that follows.

---

## Psychological Suspense

Fear should come from uncertainty rather than excessive jump scares.

The game should emphasize:

* Atmosphere.
* Anticipation.
* Isolation.
* Subtle environmental changes.
* Player imagination.

Suspense should steadily increase throughout the experience.

---

## Environmental Storytelling

The world should communicate its story through:

* The environment.
* Objects.
* Layout.
* Lighting.
* Audio.
* Player discovery.

Players should feel rewarded for paying attention to details.

---

## Player Curiosity

Curiosity should drive exploration.

Questions should naturally arise as players notice inconsistencies within an otherwise familiar environment.

The game should encourage players to investigate without forcing them.

---

# Design Philosophy

Afterschool Cleanup follows several guiding principles.

* Ordinary situations can become extraordinary experiences.
* Atmosphere is more powerful than spectacle.
* Every environment should tell a story.
* Suspense should build gradually.
* Exploration should feel rewarding.
* Players should remain emotionally engaged through curiosity.

The game aims to create lasting psychological tension rather than short-lived shocks.

---

# Artistic Direction

The visual presentation should emphasize realism, readability, and atmosphere.

Key artistic goals include:

* Believable school environments.
* Strong environmental lighting.
* Subtle visual storytelling.
* Clean environmental composition.
* Clear interaction feedback.

Visual design should support immersion without overwhelming the player.

---

# Audio Direction

Sound is a primary storytelling tool.

The audio design should include:

* Ambient environmental sounds.
* Realistic cleaning effects.
* Spatial audio cues.
* Carefully controlled silence.
* Atmospheric musical support.

Audio should heighten tension while reinforcing the player's surroundings.

---

# Technical Goals

Afterschool Cleanup should prioritize:

* Stable performance.
* Responsive first-person controls.
* Immersive interaction systems.
* Efficient environmental rendering.
* Modular gameplay systems.
* Reliable save functionality.

Technical implementation should remain invisible to the player, allowing the experience to remain immersive.

# Final Principle

Afterschool Cleanup is built on the idea that the familiar can become unsettling when viewed through a different perspective.

By transforming an ordinary school cleaning routine into a carefully crafted psychological thriller, the game aims to leave players questioning what they saw, what they missed, and what may still be waiting after the final classroom has been cleaned.

Sometimes the quietest moments leave the strongest impressions.
