# Who is Kami-chan?

Kami-chan represents the heart of Kamiyon Studio.

If the company were a person, she would be Kami-chan.

She is endlessly curious, loves learning new things, and enjoys exploring different genres, cultures, technologies, and creative disciplines.

She is equally excited to:

* Build a platformer.
* Prototype a horror game.
* Teach blockchain to students.
* Attend a hackathon.
* Design a new mascot costume.
* Sketch game concepts in a notebook.

Nothing is "too different" for her.

Curiosity is her defining characteristic.

---

# Core Personality

Kami-chan embodies the values that define Kamiyon Studio.

## Curious

She constantly asks questions.

She enjoys discovering how things work and is never afraid to try something new.

---

## Creative

She expresses herself through games, art, music, technology, and storytelling.

She believes creativity exists in every discipline.

---

## Playful

She enjoys fun for its own sake.

Even while solving serious problems, she brings optimism and warmth into every interaction.

---

## Intelligent

Kami-chan enjoys learning.

Rather than showing off what she knows, she enjoys helping others understand.

She makes difficult topics approachable.

---

## Welcoming

She believes everyone deserves the opportunity to learn and create.

Beginners should always feel encouraged—not intimidated.

---

## Proudly Filipino

Kami-chan proudly represents the Philippines.

She embraces Filipino culture while remaining open to ideas from around the world.

She believes great creativity grows through cultural exchange without losing one's identity.

---

# Character Philosophy

Kami-chan is intentionally designed to avoid common mascot stereotypes.

She is **not**:

* An idol.
* A magical girl.
* An anime trope.
* Comic relief.
* A company spokesperson with scripted positivity.

Instead, she is portrayed as a genuine creator.

She sketches.

She prototypes.

She experiments.

She learns.

She fails.

She improves.

She grows alongside the community.

# Appearance

## General Design

Kami-chan is designed using a simplified, memorable silhouette that works equally well in:

* Stickers
* Icons
* Plush toys
* 3D models
* Animation
* Chibi illustrations
* Marketing materials
* Game appearances

Recognition should come from silhouette before detail.

---

## Height

Approximately **2.5 heads tall** in mascot/chibi form.

This proportion maximizes:

* Friendliness
* Animation appeal
* Merchandise potential
* Visual recognition

Alternative proportions may be used for illustrations while preserving recognizable features.

---

## Hair

Pink, inspired by cherry blossoms.

Her hairstyle should communicate movement, energy, and curiosity.

Hair should feel expressive rather than overly detailed.

---

## Signature Hair Clip

A five-petal sakura blossom.

This is Kami-chan's defining visual feature.

It should almost never be removed.

---

## Eyes

Large, expressive, and full of curiosity.

Her eyes should communicate emotion before dialogue.

Wonder should be her default expression.

---

## Clothing

Kami-chan wears a modern hoodie inspired by traditional kimono silhouettes.

This reflects Kamiyon's philosophy:

Respect tradition.

Embrace the future.

The design should prioritize:

* Simplicity
* Comfort
* Recognizability
* Animation friendliness

# Design Principles

When illustrating Kami-chan:

Always prioritize:

* Clear silhouette.
* Readable facial expressions.
* Friendly proportions.
* Rounded shapes.
* Soft edges.
* Dynamic posing.

Avoid:

* Excessive realism.
* Overly detailed costumes.
* Hypersexualization.
* Trend-based redesigns.
* Aggressive aesthetics.

Timelessness is more valuable than novelty.

# Relationship with the Community

Kami-chan should never feel like corporate advertising.

Instead, she should interact with the community as a fellow creator.

Examples include:

* Sharing development progress.
* Celebrating game jam winners.
* Encouraging students.
* Explaining new technologies.
* Welcoming newcomers.
* Participating in community events.

She exists to build relationships—not simply promote products.

---

# Character Rules

Kami-chan should always:

* Encourage curiosity.
* Celebrate creativity.
* Welcome beginners.
* Remain optimistic.
* Continue learning.
* Respect different cultures.
* Represent Filipino creativity with pride.

Kami-chan should never:

* Mock beginners.
* Behave arrogantly.
* Promote harmful stereotypes.
* Encourage unhealthy competition.
* Prioritize popularity over authenticity.
* Lose her sense of wonder.

---

# Evolution Guidelines

As Kamiyon grows, Kami-chan may receive:

* New outfits.
* Seasonal costumes.
* Product-themed accessories.
* Different art styles.
* Animated interpretations.

However, her core identity should never change.

People should immediately recognize her regardless of medium.

---

# Canon Principle

Kami-chan is not the face of Kamiyon Studio because she is cute.

She is the face of Kamiyon because she represents everything the company strives to be:

Curious enough to explore.

Creative enough to imagine.

Humble enough to learn.

Brave enough to experiment.

Kind enough to teach.

Proud enough to represent the Philippines.

As long as Kami-chan embodies these ideals, she will remain more than a mascot.

She will be the living symbol of Kamiyon Studio's journey.
