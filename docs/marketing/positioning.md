# Purpose

This document defines how Kamiyon Studio positions itself within the market.

It establishes who we are, who we serve, what differentiates us, and how we should communicate our value to clients, partners, investors, communities, and future team members.

This positioning should remain consistent across marketing materials, presentations, proposals, and public communications.

---

# Positioning Statement

**Kamiyon Studio is a multidisciplinary interactive experience studio that develops games, educational technologies, gamified platforms, and digital solutions that combine creativity, technology, and thoughtful design to create meaningful impact.**

Unlike studios that specialize exclusively in entertainment or software development, Kamiyon bridges multiple disciplines to build experiences that are both engaging and purposeful.

---

# Who We Are

Kamiyon Studio is:

* A game development studio.
* An interactive experience studio.
* A creative technology company.
* An educational technology partner.
* A gamification specialist.
* A multidisciplinary development team.

We operate at the intersection of creativity, technology, education, and innovation.

---

# What We Build

Kamiyon develops:

* Original games.
* Educational games.
* Gamified learning platforms.
* Interactive web applications.
* Mobile applications.
* MVPs and prototypes.
* Blockchain-enabled experiences.
* AI-assisted digital solutions.
* UI/UX design systems.
* Creative digital products.

Regardless of the medium, every product should reflect our commitment to thoughtful design and meaningful experiences.

---

# Who We Serve

Kamiyon primarily serves:

* Educational institutions.
* Startups.
* Businesses.
* Government organizations.
* Nonprofit organizations.
* Web3 organizations.
* Communities seeking innovative digital experiences.

Our original products are primarily designed for students and casual gamers, while our services support organizations looking to solve meaningful problems through interactive technology.

---

# Our Place in the Market

Kamiyon does not compete solely as:

* A software agency.
* A game outsourcing studio.
* An educational technology company.
* A blockchain consultancy.

Instead, we occupy the intersection of these disciplines.

Our ability to combine expertise across multiple fields allows us to approach problems from perspectives that traditional agencies often cannot.

Kamiyon Studio is more than a game development company and more than a software development studio.
