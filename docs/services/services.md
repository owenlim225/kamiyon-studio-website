# Purpose

This document defines the official service offerings of Kamiyon Studio.

It serves as the primary reference for understanding what the studio offers, who those services are for, and how they support Kamiyon's long-term mission of creating games and interactive experiences that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.

While our services generate revenue, they also help us build expertise, strengthen partnerships, and fund the development of our original intellectual property.

---

# Service Philosophy

We do not simply deliver software.

We collaborate with clients to solve problems through thoughtful design, technology, and interactive experiences.

Every engagement should provide long-term value rather than a short-term deliverable.

Our role is to become a trusted creative and technical partner.
