# Purpose

This document defines the primary industries and sectors that Kamiyon Studio serves.

Rather than targeting every possible market, Kamiyon focuses on industries where games, interactivity, technology, and thoughtful design can create measurable value.

These industries align with our mission of creating experiences that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.

---

# Industry Selection Philosophy

We choose industries based on impact rather than size.

The best opportunities are those where interactive experiences can:

* Improve learning.
* Increase engagement.
* Simplify complex ideas.
* Encourage participation.
* Solve meaningful problems.
* Create memorable experiences.

If interactive design is capable of producing better outcomes, it is a market worth exploring.

# Ideal Project Characteristics

The projects most aligned with Kamiyon typically involve one or more of the following:

* Gamification

* Long-term collaboration

---

# Industries Outside Our Focus

Kamiyon is intentionally selective.

We generally avoid projects where:

* Technology is requested without a clear purpose.
* User experience is treated as an afterthought.
* Ethical concerns outweigh potential value.
* Collaboration is unlikely to succeed.
* The work does not align with our mission or values.

While exceptions may exist, strategic alignment takes priority over short-term opportunities.

# Why These Industries?

Our target industries share a common characteristic:

They all benefit from better engagement.

Whether teaching students, onboarding blockchain users, training employees, or inspiring communities, interactive experiences consistently improve how people learn, participate, and remember.

That is where Kamiyon creates the greatest value.

# Success Metrics

Success within an industry is not measured solely by revenue.

We also evaluate:

* Educational outcomes.
* User engagement.
* Community growth.
* Long-term partnerships.
* Portfolio quality.
* Opportunities to create lasting impact.

The right partnerships should strengthen both our clients and Kamiyon Studio.

# Final Principle

Kamiyon Studio does not seek to work with every industry.

We seek to work with industries where creativity, technology, and thoughtful design can genuinely improve people's experiences.

By focusing our efforts where we create the most value, we remain true to our mission:

To create games and interactive experiences that educate, inspire, and make a lasting impact.
